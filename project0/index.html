I am doing mountain climbing every weekend.
Every weekend, actually every Sunday morning, I go out with my backpack and two bottles of water to climb the mountain Kwan-Ack. Kwan-Ack San(mountain) is located in southern suburbs of Seoul City and Northern part of Kwacheon City in Kyunggi province.
